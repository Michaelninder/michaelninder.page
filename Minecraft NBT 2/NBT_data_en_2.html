Examples of NBT-Data in Minecraft

_________________________________.___._.___._________________________________
CommandFunctionNBT
/give @a Stick{Enchantments:[{id:"minecraft:knockback", lvl:255s}]} Gives all players a Stick with the "Knockback1000" enchantment The NBT tag here is the enchantment
/give @a Stick[Tag=Test] Gives all players a Stick with a tag named "Test" The NBT tag here is the tag named Test
/give @a Sculk_catalyst[Tag=Tp] Gives all players a Sculk Catalyst with the tag "Tp" The NBT tag here is the tag named Tp
/give @a "blockname"[tag="your Tag"] Gives all players a specific block with a specific tag The NBT tag here is the specific tag
/enchant @a minecraft:unbreaking 3 Enchants the item held by all players with durability 3 The NBT tag Enchantment: Durability 3 is added
