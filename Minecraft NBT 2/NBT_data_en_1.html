Examples of NBT Data in Minecraft

_________________________________.___._.___._________________________________
Command Function NBT
/give @a Stick{Enchantments:[{id:"minecraft:knockback", lvl:255s}]} gives all players a
Stick with the enchantment
"Knockback1000"		 The NBT tag here is
the enchantment
/give @a Stick[Tag=Test] gives all players a Stick
with a tag named "Test"		 The NBT tag here is the
tag named Test
/give @a Sculk_catalyst[Tag=Tp] gives all players a Sculk Catalyst
with the tag "Tp"		 The NBT tag here is the
tag named Tp
/give @a "blockname"[tag="your tag"] gives all players a specific
block with a specific tag		 The NBT tag here is the
specific tag
/enchant @a minecraft:unbreaking 3 enchants the item held by all
players with durability 3		 The NBT tag Enchantment:
Durability 3 is added
/enchant >at whom< >Enchantment< >level<
